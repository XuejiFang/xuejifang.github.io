Xueji is a joint CS Ph.D. Student at Zhejiang University and Westlake University, advised by Dr. Guo-Jun Qi and co-adivised by Dr. Liyuan Ma. Prior to that, he obtained Bachelor degree at Shanghai University advised by Dr. Guangtai Ding.

Research

His research aims to make generative models more controllable and efficient. While his explorations include Customization Generation, Generative Foundation Models and Test-Time Training, he is currently focused on (Discrete) Diffusion Reasoning .
